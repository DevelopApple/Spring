Installation

  1. Download the .ipa file and Cydia Impactor
  2. Open Cydia Impactor and connect your device
  3. Drag the Spring.ipa file onto the Cydia Impactor window
  4. Enter your Apple ID (email and password)
  5. Open Settings → General → Profiles & Device Management
  6. Trust the new certificate → open Spring
  7. Tap 'Jailbreak' and enjoy!

Possible Touch ID fix:

    If Touch ID doesn’t work after Jailbreak with Spring10 try this:

  1. Reboot your Device
  2. Turn off TouchID in Settings
  3. Jailbreak your Device
  4. Turn on TouchID in Settings
